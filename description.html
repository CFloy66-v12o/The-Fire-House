Welcome! Odds are, you have never been in a firehouse before, so, we are bringing all the experience you would find in one, to you. There are many areas of expertise that are of great benefit to the general public. This Q&A platform aims to put that expertise at your fingertips, to let you tap into that knowledge base, if you will. The platform is set up with channels indicated by a hash symbol, (ex.) #fire extinguishers, where you would expect to find conversations / answers relating to fire extinguishers, and their seemingly endless NFPA guidelines re: care, placement, how many per structure / per floor, etc. you will find certified professionals, in a fire department, who can quote the code book. So business owners and, homeowners alike can find advice about fire extinguishers

All of our moderators are certified Fire Department personnel

so you know you are getting information you can trust. Keep in mind, the information is not legal info and in no way should replace calling 911 in situations that warrant it! this is strictly for advice, meant for non-emergency situations.
