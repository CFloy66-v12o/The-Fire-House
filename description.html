Description

So, anyone who's spent time in a fire house knows about the "Dinner Table". It's clearly not just a place to consume meals, it's much more than that. It's a place to gather all hours and tell stories, conduct training drills, discuss politics, chapel time etc. That's what this platform hopes to provide.

A virtual Fire House "Dinner Table" where personnel can gather to discuss trending topics, share ideas, express concerns, and otherwise...solve the world's problems, all within "The Fire House".
