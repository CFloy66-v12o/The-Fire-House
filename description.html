The Fire House®

~Description

So, anyone who's spent time in a fire house knows about the "Dinner Table". It's definately not just a place to consume meals, it's much more than that. It's a place to gather all hours and tell stories, conduct training drills, discuss politics, hold chapel etc. This platform aims to provide that same atmosphere, albeit in a virtual environment..

Your virtual Fire House "Dinner Table".
